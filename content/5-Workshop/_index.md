# Smart Image Platform Workshop

The workshop contains two deployment/reference paths and a product demonstration:

1. **AWS CDK deployment:** the primary path, based on the `AWS-Project` implementation and the source of truth when a difference exists.
2. **AWS Console reference (optional):** a visual explanation of the resources and settings created by CDK. These steps may be simplified and should not be repeated in the same account and Region after deploying the CDK stacks.
3. **Product showcase and verification:** presents the live-domain deployment, observed features, and their correlation with AWS resources.

Examples and screenshots use Region `ap-southeast-1` and the `staging` environment. Physical names may contain suffixes generated by CDK or CloudFormation.

> **Important:** Do not mix manually created resources with CDK-managed resources in the same environment. CDK does not automatically manage or delete resources created separately in the Console.
